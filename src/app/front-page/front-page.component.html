ODETTE MUREKATETE

Odette is the leader of Rambagirakawa women association with 289 members. This women association is part of the coffee farmers’ cooperative Dukundekawa Musasa.

I sensitized other women to form a group that would make us come together to share valuable ideas, counseling, and create job opportunities for ourselves during the coffee off-season period.

We got the cooperative's approval, formed the group in 2011 and named it "Rambagirakawa" deriving from a Kinyarwanda verb “Kurambagira” meaning to spread everywhere.

We chose that name because the coffee was traditionally considered to be owned by men only. So we wanted the coffee to widen and get to us women as well. This became reality as now we see our coffee has spread and reached far beyond Africa!
